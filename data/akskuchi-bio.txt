Aditya K Surikuchi is a Master's graduate in Machine learning and Artificial intelligence from Aalto University, Finland. Computer vision use-cases, language understanding, and information retrieval are his primary areas of interest. At Aalto, he worked as a research assistant with the CBIR group, focusing on Deep captioning and Visual storytelling. Before pursuing masters, he worked as a Software Engineer for Boomerang Commerce (now CommerceIQ) and GGK Technologies. He received his Bachelor's degree with distinction in Computer Science and Engineering from Amrita University, India, during which he represented the university and country at the ACM ICPC world finals in 2014.
